Frito Pie

Description

Frito Pie, pie but with Frito chips with chilli, cheese, and other toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Seasoning
  • Fritos
  • Beans
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and onions together
  2. mix in seasoning
  3. pour in bean
  4. Mix 3/4 bag of frito into bottom of a casserole dish
  5. sprinkle on cheese
  6. bake until melty cheese
