Fried egg

description

Fried egg using oil

ingredeints

  1. egg
  2. Virgin free olive oil

steps

  1. wash egg
  2. crack egg onto pan that's been heated up a few minutes prior and has olive oil on it
  3. let egg sit for a few minutes
  4. flip egg over with spatula
  5. let cook a few more minutes
  6. eat
